# Returning a Game (with a Barcode Scanner)

Use this guide when your station has a barcode scanner. The barcode return process works a lot like the barcode borrow process — scan the game, scan the patron, confirm.

---

## What You Need

- A barcode scanner connected to your station.
- A game with a barcode label on the box.
- The patron's badge (it has a barcode on it).

---

## Steps

### 1. Go to the Return page

<!-- TODO: screenshot — Return_Page.png (barcode field visible) -->
![The Return page with the Barcode scan text field visible in the toolbar](img/Return_Page.png)

Select the **Return** tab in the navigation bar at the **top right of the screen** — or press **Tab** on your keyboard until **Return** is selected, then press **Enter**.

You will see a **Barcode** scan text field in the toolbar near the top of the page.

---

### 2. Scan the game's barcode

Point the scanner at the barcode label on the game box and scan it.

A return pop-up window will appear automatically.

---

### 3. The return window opens

<!-- TODO: screenshot — Return_Modal.png -->
![The return pop-up window showing the game title and a patron barcode field](img/Return_Modal.png)

The window will show the name of the game. A field will be ready for you to scan the patron's badge.

---

### 4. Scan the patron's badge

Point the scanner at the barcode on the patron's badge and scan it. If the barcode matches the return, the patron's name will be filled in automatically.

---

### 5. Confirm the return was successful

A success banner (shown in green) will appear at the **bottom of the screen** confirming the return was completed. It will close on its own after a moment.

The game will no longer appear in the list. The return is complete.

---

## Troubleshooting

**An error banner (shown in red) appears with the message "No checked out games with this barcode":**
This means the game was not recorded as checked out in the system. This was likely a mistake made during the borrow process. Take the game back from the patron, even though the app shows an error. Then close the error banner by clicking the **X** on it or pressing **Escape**. Let someone know so the borrow record can be looked into.

**The scanner does not seem to be working:**
Click directly on the **Barcode** scan text field on the page (or inside the return window), then try scanning again. Clicking the field first makes sure the scanner knows where to send the barcode.

---

## Notes

- The return window will always appear when you scan a game, even if only one copy is checked out.
- If an error banner (shown in red) appears at the bottom of the screen and the reason is not clear, ask for help or write down what you were doing so someone can look into it later.
